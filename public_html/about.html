About Me

Hi! I’m Marah Farroukh, a passionate and driven Frontend Web Designer with a love for crafting visually appealing and user-friendly websites. My journey into web development began with a deep fascination for design and technology.

My background in computer science has provided me with a strong foundation in various programming languages and frameworks, but my true passion lies in front-end development. I enjoy turning ideas into reality, bringing designs to life with code, and ensuring every user interaction is smooth and intuitive.

Throughout my journey, I have worked on several exciting projects that challenged my creativity and problem-solving skills. From building responsive websites to optimizing user experiences for different devices, I’m constantly learning and growing in the ever-evolving world of web development.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me exploring new design trends, learning about the latest technologies, or collaborating with other developers to build innovative solutions. I'm always open to new opportunities to expand my knowledge and contribute to meaningful projects.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out if you'd like to work together on a project or just chat about web design and development!
